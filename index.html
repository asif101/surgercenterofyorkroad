State of the art surgical care

The Surgical Center of York Road is a state of the art outpatient facility featuring the most advanced technologies available in a personalized setting. Our facility is utilized by leading local physicians, and provides world class surgical care to patients.

The Surgery Center of York Road provides care in multiple medical specialties:

  • Vascular Surgery
  • Plastic/Reconstructive
  • Urology
  • Gastroenterology
  • Gynecology
  • General Surgery

The Board Certified surgeons provide patients with high quality care in a safe environment.  Our Nursing clinical staff put the patient first, with personalized care for each patient. Anesthesia services are provided by board certified Anesthesiologists specializing in outpatient anesthesia.  In addition, our modern recovery room is designed with your personal comfort and safety in mind. 

Located in Lutherville, MD right off the 695 beltway, the Surgery Center of York Road is easily accessible for patient and family convenience.
